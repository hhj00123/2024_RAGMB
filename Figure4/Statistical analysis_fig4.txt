The statistical significance of the normally distributed data of physiological and biochemical, were assessed by analysis of variance followed by Dunnett’s multiple comparisons test. Differences were considered significant when p < 0.05. **** indicates p < 0.0001; *** indicates p < 0.001; ** indicates p < 0.01; and * indicates p < 0.05. In addition to the above, GraphPad Prism version 9.0 was used for visualization of the results.
